About

Originally from a small town in Virginia, I always felt that I belonged in a city where there was a lot of diversity and different walks of life. With a strong desire to fulfill my dream, I worked and saved as fast and hard as I could, stuffed my car to the brim with my belongings and started my journey to sunny Los Angeles, California.
When I arrived to LA, I didn't know what type of career I wanted to pursue. Although I had years of experience in sales my heart just wasn't into it anymore. My S.O. saw how frustrating it was for me to choose a new path and pushed me to try dabbling in HTML and CSS and lo and behold I fell in LOVE.
There is so much you can teach yourself and I just had an urge to learn more within a shorter time period so I enrolled into General Assembly with gave me a strong foundation to continue grow as a Full Stack Web Developer
